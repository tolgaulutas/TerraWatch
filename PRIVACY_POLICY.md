# TerraWatch Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

TerraWatch is a wildfire and earthquake alert app for farmers and landowners.
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data
we collect and how we use it.

---

## Data We Collect

### Location Data
TerraWatch requests access to your device's location to:
- Send nearby wildfire and earthquake notifications
- Display relevant weather forecasts for your fields

Location data is processed **only on your device**. It is never transmitted
to our servers or stored externally.

### Field Data
Field boundaries and names you create are stored **locally on your device**
using Apple's standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

### Purchase Information
In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple. TerraWatch does not collect
or store any payment information.

---

## Third-Party Services

TerraWatch uses the following external APIs to provide its features.
Your approximate location (bounding box coordinates) may be sent to these
services to retrieve relevant data:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| NASA FIRMS | Wildfire detection data | https://www.nasa.gov/privacy |
| USGS Earthquake Hazards | Earthquake data | https://www.usgs.gov/privacy-policy |
| Open-Meteo | Weather forecast data | https://open-meteo.com/en/terms |
| Sentinel Hub | Satellite imagery (NDVI) | https://www.sentinel-hub.com/tos |

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties
- We do not store any data on our own servers

---

## Data Retention

All app data (fields, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your
device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

---

## Children's Privacy

TerraWatch does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
